At Dianuel Tiles, we believe that innovation begins with information. Explore our latest insights and research updates on sustainable tile and cement production. Cement
Rethinking Cement

Traditional cement is costly and polluting. Our research explores alternative combinations using limestone, clay, silica, and pozzolans to create a more climate-friendly cement — made by Cameroonians, for Africa.

Resource
From Rock to Resource

What if the key to Africa’s construction future lies beneath our feet? We’re analyzing locally sourced rocks like quartz and rutile to unlock materials that meet international green building standards.
